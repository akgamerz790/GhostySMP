Ghost Abilities (by Tier)
The Draugr (Mid Tier)

    Ability name: Super Crush

    How it works: On spawn, deals 77 hearts of damage to a specific targeted player (not the ghost owner).

    Duration: Instant (on spawn)

    Cooldown: 15 seconds

The Poltergeist (High Tier)

    Ability name: Object Throw

    How it works: For 15 seconds, throws any nearby blocks within a 10-block radius from its current location, excluding the ghost owner.

    Duration: 15 seconds

    Cooldown: 15 seconds

The Anchors (Low Tier)

    Ability name: Harm of Weight

    How it works: If a player enters a 99-block radius:

        Jump height becomes 00

        Movement speed becomes 20%20%

        Ender pearls cannot be used
        (All effects exclude the ghost owner.)

    Duration: Not specified

    Cooldown: 15 seconds

The Disruptors (Mid Tier)

    Ability name: Mind Control

    How it works: For 15 seconds, if a player is within a 10-block radius and tries to hit the ghost owner, 90%90% of that damage is reflected back onto the attacker (excluding the ghost owner).

    Duration: 15 seconds

    Cooldown: 15 seconds

The Shifter (High Tier)

    Ability name: Air Pearl

    How it works: When aiming at a specific player:

        Teleports the target 5050 blocks into the air

        Locks all movement/actions (placing blocks, using mace, using ender pearls, MLG, etc.)
        (Excludes the ghost owner.)

    Duration: Not specified

    Cooldown: 15 seconds

The Infector (Low Tier)

    Ability name: Poison Fortress

    How it works: If a player comes within a 77-block radius, poison is applied until the player has taken 99 hearts of damage total (excluding the ghost owner).

    Duration: Until 99 hearts damage is dealt

    Cooldown: 15 seconds

The Drainer (High Tier)

    Ability name: Stack of Blood

    How it works: When aiming at a specific player:

        Instantly deals 10 hearts of damage

        Then deals 11 heart of damage every second for 10 seconds
        (Excludes the ghost owner.)

    Duration: 10 seconds (damage-over-time portion)

    Cooldown: 3030 seconds

The Scavenger (Mid Tier)

    Ability name: The God Bow

    How it works: On use, a custom bow appears in the inventory for 15 seconds. The bow does normal damage but includes enchantments like Flame, Punch, and Multishot.

    Duration: 15 seconds

    Cooldown: 15 seconds
